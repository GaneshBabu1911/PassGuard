Two-Factor Authentication

A strong password is just the first layer. Enable 2FA to make your accounts virtually impenetrable even if your password is stolen.

Choose a 2FA Method

📱
Authenticator App (TOTP)
Time-based codes from an app like Google Authenticator or Authy. Works offline.
Security: Very High · Recommended
🔑
Hardware Security Key
Physical key (YubiKey, Google Titan). Phishing-resistant. The gold standard.
Security: Maximum
🪪
Passkeys (FIDO2)
Biometric-backed cryptographic credentials. No password needed at all.
Security: Maximum · Future-proof
💬
SMS / Email OTP
One-time codes sent via text or email. Easy to set up but vulnerable to SIM-swap.
Security: Basic · Use as last resort
